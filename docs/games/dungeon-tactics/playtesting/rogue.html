Pass-through
moves through enemies
Passthrough: You may move through enemy tiles — you just can't end your move on one. This is what makes every other action possible.

Your turn

You have 6 MP and 2 Actions. Spend them in any order — move-act-move-act is the intended rhythm. Each step costs 1 MP. Unused MP is lost at end of turn.

Actions

Backstab Attack Adjacent
5 damage if you have moved ≥ 3 tiles so far this turn.
Only 2 damage if you haven't moved enough yet.
The Rogue's core loop: move through the enemy's space to rack up tiles, then Backstab for 5. Don't throw it away for 2.
Throw Dagger Attack Range 4 (LOS)
2 damage.
Good for finishing a low-HP target from safety, or as your second action after Backstab when you've already moved away.
Quick Step Special Self
Immediately refill +3 MP.
Keeps the chain going. Use it as action 1 mid-move to fuel a long flanking run, then Backstab as action 2. You can't restore beyond the turn's starting 6.
Mark Utility Range 4 (LOS)
Place a marked token on a target. The next hit it takes from anyone deals +2 damage. One mark at a time.
Combo with the Berserker or Ranger. Mark, then let them proc it. Mark + Backstab on the same target is also solid (2 actions though).
Caltrops Utility Within 2 (empty tile)
Drop a caltrops token on an empty tile. The first enemy to enter that tile takes 1 damage and stops (ends its move).
Good chokepoint denial. Lay them in doorways or corridors the enemy AI must walk through.

Sample turn

Setup: Two brutes are adjacent; you're 3 tiles away. MP: 6, moved-tally: 0.
Step 1: Move 4 tiles, passing through one brute (passthrough) and landing behind it. MP now 2, moved-tally = 4. ✓ Backstab condition met.
Step 2: Backstab the brute for 5. (Action 1 used.)
Step 3: Quick Step → +3 MP, now 5. (Action 2 used.)
Step 4: Move 3 tiles away to safety. MP: 2 left, discarded at end of turn.
Both actions used, out of the fire. Turn ends.
