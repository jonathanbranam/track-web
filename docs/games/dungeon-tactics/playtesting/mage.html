Mana economy

Start: 6 Mana on turn 1.
Each turn: +2 Mana at the start of your turn (before acting).
Cap: 10 Mana maximum. Excess is lost — don't skip turns.
Decision: bank for Fireball (5), or chip with Firebolts (2 each)?

Your turn

Gain +2 Mana (up to cap 10). Then: Blink once (free) anywhere within 5 tiles you have LOS to, and cast spells until you run out of Mana or choose to stop. Blink may be used before or after casting.

Spells & actions

Blink FREE · 1×/turn Move Any empty tile within 5 (LOS to destination)
Teleport to any empty tile within 5. Ignores all units and objects in between. You must have LOS to the destination tile (can't land past a solid wall).
Use Blink to escape, reposition for Fireball coverage, or duck behind a wall after a big spell. It's free — use it every turn.
Firebolt 2 Mana Attack Range 6 (LOS)
3 damage to a single target.
Efficient mana spend (1.5 dmg per mana). When you can't Fireball, two Firebolts = 6 damage across two targets for 4 Mana.
Frostbite 3 Mana Attack Range 6 (LOS)
2 damage and place a frozen token: target skips its next move.
The control spell. Freeze a brute that's about to close on you, then Blink away. Also combos with Berserker's Shove — a frozen target can't step away first.
Fireball 5 Mana Attack · Area Aim a tile within 6 (LOS); hits a 3×3 area (all 9 tiles)
3 damage to every unit in the 3×3 — friend or foe. Also ignites forest tiles.
⚠ Friendly fire! Make sure no PCs are in the blast. The Mage included.
The payoff for banking. Works best when 3+ enemies are clustered. Blink to a ridge with LOS first, then fire.
Conjure Wall 3 Mana Utility Up to 3 connected empty tiles within 4
Create wall tokens on up to 3 connected empty tiles. Walls block movement and LOS. Max one wall (3 tiles) on the map at a time — a new Conjure Wall removes the old one.
Redirect enemy pathing, cut off an archer's LOS, or protect your back while casting. Don't conjure it and immediately walk behind it — enemies may path around.

Sample turn

Setup: Mana 6. Start of your turn → +2 Mana = 8. Three brutes are clustered 5 tiles north.
Step 1: Blink 5 tiles onto a ridge with LOS to all three brutes.
Step 2: Fireball (−5 Mana) → 3 damage to all three brutes. Mana: 3.
Step 3: Firebolt (−2 Mana) → 3 more to a surviving brute. Mana: 1.
Turn ends with 1 Mana, fully exposed. Next turn you'll want a wall, a Frostbite to freeze an attacker, or a Blink out. Start saving for Fireball again.
