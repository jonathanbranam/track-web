# Product Brief: Holistic Life & Productivity Management System

## Vision
A comprehensive, markdown-native solution for life management and planning that helps users align daily actions with long-term values and goals, track healthy behaviors, and maintain awareness of both immediate tasks and long-term commitments.

## Core Problem
Users struggle to connect their daily work and habits to their larger life goals, and lack a unified place to track multi-scale planning (annual, quarterly, weekly, daily) alongside recurring maintenance tasks and long-term projects.

## Key Concepts

### Planning Hierarchy
- **Life Goals & Values** — foundational layer defining what matters
- **Annual Plans** — yearly objectives and themes
- **Quarterly Plans** — 3-month focused initiatives
- **Weekly Plans** — week-level priorities and goals
- **Daily Plans** — day-specific tasks and focus

### Tracking & Reflection
- **Healthy Behaviors** — user-committed habits (e.g., exercise, meditation, sleep)
- **Daily Reflection** — end-of-day review of what was accomplished and how it went
- **Long-term Projects** — multi-month initiatives (e.g., house remodel, yard work)
- **Recurring Tasks** — maintenance tasks at any cadence (e.g., change air filter every 6 months, spring garden prep)

## Core Data Structure
- **Format**: Markdown files, compatible with Obsidian and any markdown editor
- **Goal**: Files are editable by humans, parseable by tools, and portable across ecosystems

## Initial Assumptions (to be validated)
- Single user per system (or multi-user scope TBD)
- Markdown files as source of truth
- Some form of interface/tooling exists (UI, CLI, web app, or purely file-based — TBD)
