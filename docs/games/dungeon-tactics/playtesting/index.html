The complete rulebook: core rules, terrain, all four archetypes, enemies, scenarios, and what to watch for.

🗺️ Maps

Visual reference for both scenarios — Map A "The Pool" (8×8) and Map B "The Long Field" (8×10), with terrain and starting positions.

🎴 Quick-ref cards & live trackers

Per-archetype action cards with interactive HP, Mana, MP, ammo, and action trackers. A "New Turn" button resets per-turn state; everything auto-saves in your browser.

The four builds under test

  • Berserker (Fighter) — moves through attacks; shoves enemies into walls/each other.
  • Rogue (Skirmisher) — huge mobility, passes through enemies; damage earned by moving.
  • Ranger (Kiter) — shoot-and-scoot; must move between shots; rotates ammo.
  • Mage (Artillery) — blinks, banks/spends Mana on spells, reshapes terrain; fragile.
