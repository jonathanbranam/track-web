# Dungeon Tactics

## Notes from Into the Breach for inspiration

These are general notes, we will not copy these behaviors exactly

- attacks:
  * can launch attacks over other units several tiles away
  * some are laser - all the way (or w/range) in a line
    + does not pierce
  * some are specific: 2 squares in a line
  * melee attack with reach could hit two square
  * attack hits three tiles in front of PC
  * attack all four adjacent tiles
- artillery:
  * attack in a line with range, not next to itself
  * hits in a tile
  * does knockback on adjacent tiles
- enemies could be flying
- enemies can be knocked into each other or structures, damaging both
- smoke cancels enemy attack
- movement:
  * flying
  * enemy can jump / fly over people
- special abilities:
  * enemy grapples you so you cannot move (if adjacent)
  * support characters add environmental effects and resistances
- damage and health:
  * enemies have 2-3 damage at start
  * attacks do 1-2 damage at start
- preview of actions shows all consequences:
  * example shows if enemy will die
  * shows enemy that will be pushed back
  * shows damage from pushback to enemy + other objects in path
