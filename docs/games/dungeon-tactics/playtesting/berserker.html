Berserker

Fighter — Kinetic

Moves through attacks. Win by slamming enemies into walls, hazards, and each other.

14
HP
2
Move (cardinal)
1
Action / turn

No resource

Your turn

Move up to 2 tiles (free, cardinal only), then take 1 Action.
Some actions carry their own movement (Charge, Whirlwind) on top of the free move.

Actions

Cleave Attack The 3 tiles on one facing (e.g. all tiles along your north edge)
3 damage to every enemy in those 3 tiles.
Best when two enemies are clustered in front of you. Declare facing before resolving.
Charge Attack + Move Straight cardinal line, up to 4 tiles
Move up to 4 tiles in a straight line to a tile adjacent to an enemy. Deal 4 damage to that enemy, then push it 1.
Bonus: +1 damage (total 5) if you moved ≥ 3 tiles.
Path must be clear. Push-1 can send them into a hazard. Pairs perfectly with a pit or water tile directly behind the target.
Shove Utility One adjacent enemy
Push the target 2 tiles directly away from you. No direct damage — the collision is the damage.
Collision rules: target stops when it hits a wall / unit / edge (takes 2; unit hit also takes 2), or a mountain (takes 3). Into a pit or water = removed. Shove is your highest-upside move when the geometry is right.
Hook Utility Within 3 — straight cardinal line, clear path
Pull the target 2 tiles toward you (or until adjacent).
Use to drag an enemy into a hazard, or to pull a soft target away from its buddies. Also a gap-closer: Hook then Whirlwind.
Whirlwind Attack + Move All 8 adjacent tiles
2 damage to every adjacent enemy, then move 1 tile.
Good when surrounded. The follow-up move lets you reposition — use it to step into a chokepoint or out of a fire tile.

What to track

  • HP — only thing on paper. Start at 14.
  • Everything else (push targets, collisions) is resolved on the map.

Sample turn

Setup: A brute stands 4 tiles north, right in front of a pit.
Step 1: Free move — step 0 tiles (save it, or use it to line up).
Step 2: Charge 4 tiles north → land adjacent to the brute → 5 damage (moved ≥ 3) and push it 1 north → it moves onto the pit → removed.
The charge used all 4 move tiles; free move is gone. Turn ends.
