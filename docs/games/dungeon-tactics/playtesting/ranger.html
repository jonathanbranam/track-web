Your turn

Up to 2 shots, but you must move ≥ 2 tiles between your first and second shot (the "scoot"). Total movement ≤ 4 tiles. You may also move before the first shot and after the last, within the budget.

Each shot uses the current ammo and advances the pointer. Net / Trap replace a shot but do not advance the ammo pointer.

Other actions (each replaces one shot)

Net Utility Range 4 (LOS)
Place a frozen/rooted token on the target. It cannot move on its next turn (it may still attack if already adjacent to a PC). Does not advance the ammo pointer.
Lock down the brute that's about to reach your squishies, then shoot it next turn while it's rooted.
Trap Utility Within 3 (empty tile)
Place a trap token on an empty tile. The first enemy to enter takes 3 damage and stops. Does not advance the ammo pointer.
Chokepoint denial — better than Caltrops (3 vs 1 damage). Combine with Net to funnel enemies through it.

Sample turn

Setup: Pointer on Explosive. Two brutes are clustered 5 tiles north. A straggler brute is 4 tiles east.
Step 1: Shoot the cluster with Explosive → 2 to target + 1 to both neighbours. Pointer advances → Normal. (Shot 1 used.)
Step 2: Move 2 tiles south (mandatory scoot ✓). Scoot check marked.
Step 3: Shoot straggler with Normal → 3 damage. Pointer advances → Poison. (Shot 2 used.)
Step 4: Move 2 tiles back behind a wall. Move budget spent (4 total).
Turn ends. Pointer sits on Poison — plan to apply it to something good next turn.
